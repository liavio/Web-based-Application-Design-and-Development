About Us

Viteri Rescue Organization started from a portal for animals in need to receive help across to country to opening our own location in Charlotte, NC. We are a rescue center that takes in pets and animals in need, and nurse them back to health before they head to their new homes. We not only rescue dogs and cats, but animals of all species and breeds!
